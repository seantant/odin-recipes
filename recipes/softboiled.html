Soft-boiled Eggs

Description

There’s something so crave-able about a perfect soft boiled egg. The tender white, the runny yolk. If you ask me, it’s something everyone should know how to make. It takes minutes, it requires 1 ingredient, and it’s an easy way to add protein and healthy fats to just about anything! Want to round out a salad or turn a slice of toast into a meal? Put a soft boiled egg on it.

Ingredients

  • 2 to 4 large eggs
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Fill a medium pot with water and bring to a boil. Using a slotted spoon lower the eggs into the water and let simmer for 7 minutes. Prepare a bowl of ice water.
  2. Remove the eggs and immediately place into the ice water. Let chill until the eggs are cool enough to handle.
  3. Carefully crack the eggs to remove the shell. You can use a spoon to loosen the egg from the shell.
  4. Season eggs with salt and pepper to taste.
